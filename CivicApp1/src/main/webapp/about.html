About CivicApp

Connecting citizens with civic authorities for a better community

Our Mission

CivicApp is built to empower citizens to raise civic issues directly with local authorities. By sharing reports with images, captions, and city information, we make it easier for authorities to identify, track, and resolve community problems efficiently.

What We Do

With CivicApp, users can:

  • Upload reports with images and captions.
  • Tag reports with relevant civic bodies or city tags.

Our Vision

We aim to create a bridge between citizens and civic bodies, ensuring that every complaint, feedback, and suggestion is heard and acted upon. Together, we can build smarter and more connected cities.
